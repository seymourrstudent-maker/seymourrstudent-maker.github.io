Hey — I'm Rapheal Ngireblekuu Seymour, a Computer Science student at Missouri University of Science and Technology with a minor in Engineering Management. I'm someone who doesn't stay in one lane.

On the technical side, I've built autonomous drone coordination systems, developed AI-driven tools for telemetry data analysis, and contributed to LLM evaluation workflows at Outlier AI. I'm comfortable working across Python, C++, C#, Lua, and JavaScript.

On the creative side, I've been deep in esports and broadcast production — casting competitive matches for the NECC, running events as Executive Manager for S&T Esports, and building out live streaming experiences.

I'm also a Team Lead on the Multirotor Design Team, a TA for CS1570, and a former NSBE senator. If it involves building, leading, or making something come alive — I'm probably in.
